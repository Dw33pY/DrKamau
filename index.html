Common Questions

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we partner with most major insurance providers including Jubilee, AAR, UAP, and CIC. Please bring your card for verification.

How long does a fertility assessment take?

The initial cycle monitoring usually takes one month to track ovulation patterns, after which we review results and plan the next steps.

Is Laparoscopy painful?

It is significantly less painful than open surgery. Most patients are discharged within 24 hours and resume light activities within a few days.
